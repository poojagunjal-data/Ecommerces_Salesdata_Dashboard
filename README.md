# Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

The Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and payment trends across different states and product categories. The dashboard provides actionable business insights that can help stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data daily, making it difficult to identify trends and measure performance efficiently. This dashboard was developed to provide a comprehensive overview of sales metrics, profit trends, and customer purchasing patterns through interactive visualizations.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profitability.
- Track monthly profit trends.
- Identify top-performing states and customers.
- Evaluate product category performance.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Monitor business KPIs using interactive filters.

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Analysis

- State-wise sales distribution.
- Customer-wise sales amount analysis.
- Category-wise quantity sold.

### Profit Analysis

- Monthly profit trend analysis.
- Profit contribution by product sub-categories.
- Identification of profitable and low-performing months.

### Customer Insights

- Top customers contributing to total sales.
- Purchasing trends across different customer segments.

### Product Analysis

- Category-wise quantity distribution.
- Sub-category profit analysis.
- Identification of high-performing products.

### Payment Mode Analysis

The dashboard analyzes customer payment preferences across:

- COD (Cash on Delivery)
- UPI
- Debit Card
- Credit Card
- EMI

### Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically filter data based on:

- Quarter (Qtr 1, Qtr 2, Qtr 3, Qtr 4)
- State-wise analysis

---

## Business Insights

- Maharashtra generated the highest sales among all states.
- Clothing is the highest-selling product category by quantity.
- COD is the most preferred payment method among customers.
- Monthly profit trends reveal seasonal fluctuations in business performance.
- Printers contribute the highest profit among product sub-categories.
- Top customers significantly influence overall sales revenue.
